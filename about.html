FAQ

What is Pete Kobasa's Youtube Channel?

On my channel, I produce thorough car reviews, where I score every vehicle I review on my Lemon List Evaluation Scale. Aside from reviews, which is the core of my YouTube channel, I post an occasional video describing my prior experiences with certain cars, some videos on traveling, or a podcast discussing personal interests.

What is petekobasa.com?

On my website, I will post articles relating to my car reviews. They will include my score, critical categories, my biggest take of the vechicle, and its relation to similar cars.

What is the future for petekobasa.com?

This is version 1.0 of petekobasa.com. This version of the website is serving as a platform for Eric to expand upon over the course of the year. Some features coming soon are a SQL database to manage articles, an improved review catalog, and a sortable lemon list.
